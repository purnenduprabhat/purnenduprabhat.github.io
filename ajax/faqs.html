  3. Why you went for M.Sc. in CS after doind B.Tech. in CSE, the usual path should be to go for M.Tech.?
    M.Sc. CS in Central University of Bihar offered the subjects I wanted to get deeper understanding of, like Automata Theory, Data Structures, Algorithms and Compilers, while M.Tech. offered a plethora of subjects which I did not want to study at the time.

  4. Why don't you go join industry?
    Academia is the breeding ground for industry. Major research contributions and projects from academia are developed into life-changing products by the industry. I am more into research, invention and prototyping in diverse domains, applying computer science to solve real-world problems, which makes academia, particularly MIET, the best place for me to be.
    Another narrative is that education is actually an industry, or, there is no future of academia if its not industrious.
